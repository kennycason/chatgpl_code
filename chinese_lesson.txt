Here are some basic Chinese words and phrases related to circuits and electronics that you may find useful:

电路 - diànlù - circuit
电子 - diànzǐ - electronics
电流 - diànliú - current
电压 - diànyā - voltage
导体 - dǎotǐ - conductor
绝缘体 - juéyuántǐ - insulator
电容器 - diànróngqì - capacitor
电阻器 - diànzǔqì - resistor
半导体 - bàndǎotǐ - semiconductor
晶体管 - jīngtǐguǎn - transistor

For example, you could say:

这个电路有一个大的电容器和几个电阻器。
- Zhège diànlù yǒu yīgè dà de diànróngqì hé jǐge diànzǔqì.
- This circuit has a large capacitor and several resistors.

这种半导体材料用于制造高性能的晶体管。
- Zhè zhǒng bàndǎotǐ cáiliào yòngyú zhìzào gāoxìngnéng de jīngtǐguǎn.
- This semiconductor material is used to make high-performance transistors.
